Lil Uzi Vert

Symere "Lil Uzi Vert" Woods is a North Philedelphia native that is making a name for himself on Billboard's Top 100 with many hit songs. He is also gainging a ton of eyes with his flamboyant fashion styles and his unique music styles.

December 18, 2015 - Uzi then released his third mixtape Luv Is Rage with productions from Don Cannon, FKi, Sonny Digital, TM88, DP Beats, Maaly Raw and more.

April 15, 2016- Uzi released his fourth mixtape Lil Uzi Vert vs. the World with production from Don Cannon, Metro Boomin, WondaGurl, M-TRAX, Smatt Sertified and more, with the cover art designed by Fvrris.>

June 13, 2016- He was named as one of the ten 2016 XXL Freshmen.

July 31, 2016- Uzi released his fifth mixtape The Perfect Luv Tape on his 22nd birthday.>

October 2016- Uzi collaborated with the rap group Migos on the single "Bad and Boujee".>

January 2017- "Bad and Boujie" reached number one on the US Billboard Hot 100, becoming Uzi Vert's first number one single as a featured artist and his highest charting single>.

March 24th, 2017- Uzi released the song XO TOUR Llif3 as a single becoming Uzi's highest charting song as a lead artist, reaching a #7 peak on the Billboard Hot 100.
